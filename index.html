Vision

In this era, I strongly believe our digital presence and the content we create can leave lifelong impacts on others. As a Game Designer, I'm currently building these experiences in Unreal Engine. A game's graphics, music, and sound design may leave a great first impression, but above all else, I strive to create fun experiences through prototypes and scripting. These fun experiences are what leave positive and lasting memories. I especially love the impression the platformer genre can give to players. Creating Lasting memories is what I hope to do every day.
